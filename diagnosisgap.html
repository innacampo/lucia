The Unseen Struggle

The Women's Mental Health Diagnosis Gap

The Gap Explained

Women are disproportionately affected by mental health conditions, yet they often face significant delays in diagnosis. This is often due to a lack of gender-specific research, healthcare provider biases, and a tendency to dismiss symptoms.

This "diagnosis gap" not only prolongs suffering but also hinders effective treatment, leading to a cycle of misdiagnosis and frustration.

How LUCIA Can Help

LUCIA empowers women to track and articulate their health journey, providing clinicians with organized, actionable data to bridge the diagnosis gap and ensure women receive the care they deserve.
